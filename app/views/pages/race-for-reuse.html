From now through December 6th let's campaign together toward a nationwide display of civic engagement at the local level.


This October, Code for America challenged civic-minded technologists to launch a civic engagement app in their community by election day. Volunteers in 24 cities across the country stepped up to the challenge.

In some cities apps support volunteer adoption programs for emergency services and public works. In other cities apps empower residents to curate locally relevant information and share ideas for future planning. In all of our Race for Reuse cities, residents can attend events and connect with other innovative citizens -- while helping their city.

Find out if there's an activity in your city and get involved.

How it works ↑ Back to top

On November 7th, Code for America and local organizing teams begin a national campaign for citizen engagement at the local level, working together to get the word out and connect people to activities where the live.

Between November 7 and December 6 teams campaign for participation in their local app activities

On Saturday December 1st we all synch up in a united display of civic engagement
