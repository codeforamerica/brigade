We just wrapped a 60 day campaign for online civic engagement and the results are in.


The Race for Reuse campaign launched in October with a stage one sprint to stand up civic engagement apps in cities across the country. In November, stage two kicked in to promote civic engagement through these new apps.

Here are the highlights of what we did

  • 31 apps in 28 cities
  • 15 LocalWiki, 12 Adopt-a, 2 Shareabouts, 1 Textizen
  • 2009 new LocalWiki pages by 416 new contributors
  • 142 fire hydrants adopted in Anchorage, AK
  • 26 shelters adopted in Raleigh, NC
