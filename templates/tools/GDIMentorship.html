How it Works

In truth, anyone anytime can join their local Code for America Brigade and hack on projects.

Join a Brigade

But this year several Brigade Captains are organizing partnerships with their local GDI Chapters to offer women with basic tech skills a chance to hack and learn alongside a specified mentor. Mentors are all volunteers (of any gender) who already hack on data at their Brigade meetups
and want to help YOU apply your knowledge while coding on projects that impact your local community.

This brilliant idea of merging Brigades with GDI came from Philly, where the Brigade Code for Philly and their local GDI Chapter piloted this mentorship program during 2014, the "Summer of Open Source." You can read about the details on the Code for America blog, among many other places like Technical.ly that picked up on the story.

Or, you can sign up below and have your own great tale to tell! Like LeeAnn, and the 10 women below, all part of the Summer of Open Source 2014.
