1. Overview

As you learned on the Brigade About page and Brigade Organize page , Brigades are local volunteer groups that bring together community members to help make government work better - and they exist all over the world! No matter their location, Brigades build technology to support local government accountability & civic engagement.

This page will dig deeper into all the nuts and bolts you need to know if you are organizing a Brigade in the US or Internationally.

You can see where Brigades are already located on the Brigade Website Map . It is best to concentrate Brigade work with one central Brigade in each location, although some Brigades participate as Regional Brigades, and invite residents from neighboring cities to participate until that city is ready to have their own Brigade. We can help you figure out what makes sense as you get started.

* US Brigades can join as Official Chapters and Network Members, International Brigades are invited to join as Network Members and can then explore becoming a Code for All partner.
