Free Software for Brigades

What CfA Offers and how you can access

Changing the world is a community effort, and we’re here to help! Some of the most cutting-edge tech companies have made their products and services available for your Brigade to use for free. These software donations enable you quickly and easily produce innovative apps.

Below, you’ll find a list of what’s available to your Brigade!

  • If you require a code, email inkinds@codeforamerica.org to request one.
  • If you’re a Brigade member, please also CC your Brigade captain on any email to inkinds@codeforamerica.org.
  • If you have any questions, send us an email or ping us on Slack (@brigade-info).

    • Thanks for everything you do - happy building!

    Cloud Hosting

    Heroku is a simple, easy-to-use cloud hosting service which is perfect for small to medium projects.

    Amazon Web Services offers server hosting and high-powered services to deploy and scale a successful project.

    Microsoft's Bizspark program offers free Microsoft software and credits for their cloud platform, Azure.

    Twilio is the easiest way to send SMS messages or handle phone calls for your project.

    Communications

    G Suite is Google's hosted product featuring Gmail, Google Drive, Groups, and more.

    Slack is a group communication tool built around chat.

    Mapping Software

    CARTO helps you easily work with and visualize geospatial data.

    Other

    GitHub offers code hosting and project management tools for open source projects.
