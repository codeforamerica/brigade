About this Priority Action Area

With something as seemingly-straightforward as providing notifications about court obligations, these projects transform the local criminal justice ecosystem by making sure people know their obligations and can easily pay fines using a modern system.

Are you working on (or looking to work on) a project in the Court Notifications area? Let Brigade leaders know by posting about it on the Brigade Network Discourse.

Projects

Court notification projects, like Courtbot, have been very effective projects in communities across the country; from Tulsa, OK to Anchorage, AK. We want to see these projects and similar ones replicated in communities across the country as part of modernizing the way our courts communicate with our community members.
